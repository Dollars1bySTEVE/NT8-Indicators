# SmartMoneyDashboard Indicator

## Description
The SmartMoneyDashboard Indicator is designed to provide traders with insights into market trends, helping them make informed decisions. This powerful tool visualizes buying and selling pressure, enabling users to identify potential trading opportunities.

## Features
- **Real-time Data Analysis**: Analyze market data in real-time to capture trends as they develop.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive design that makes it easy for traders of all experience levels to use.
- **Customizable Settings**: Tailor the indicator's parameters to fit your personal trading strategy.
- **Alerts**: Set up alerts to notify you of significant market movements.

## Installation
To install the SmartMoneyDashboard indicator:
1. Download the indicator files from the repository.
2. Open NinjaTrader 8.
3. Navigate to 'Tools' > 'Import' > 'NinjaScript Add-On'.
4. Select the `.zip` file and click 'Open' to import.
5. Restart NinjaTrader to complete the installation.

## Configuration
After installation, access the SmartMoneyDashboard settings:
1. Right-click on your chart where the indicator is applied.
2. Select 'Indicator' from the context menu.
3. Choose 'SmartMoneyDashboard' and click 'Add'.
4. Customize the settings, including color preferences, alert thresholds, and display options.

## Usage Examples
To use the SmartMoneyDashboard indicator effectively:
- **Example 1**: Identify strong buying pressure during a bullish trend and enter a long position.
- **Example 2**: Use the alert feature to notify you when the market reverses, allowing for timely exits.

## Troubleshooting
- **Issue**: The indicator does not display on the chart.
  - **Solution**: Ensure that you have added the indicator to your chart and that the data series is compatible.
  
- **Issue**: Alerts are not triggering.
  - **Solution**: Check your alert settings and ensure the conditions are realistic based on current market activity.

## Pro Tips
- Experiment with different configuration settings to find what works best for your trading style.
- Use the SmartMoneyDashboard in conjunction with other indicators for a more comprehensive analysis of market conditions.
