## Indicators List

- D1ADR Indicator

### Features
- Provides average daily range calculation based on the last 14 days.
- Helps in determining potential price movement within a trading day.

### Settings
- **Period**: The number of days to calculate the average range (default is 14).
- **Alerts**: Option to trigger alerts when the price reaches certain thresholds based on the calculated range.

### Example Usage
Configure the D1ADR in your trading platform to track average daily price fluctuations and adjust your strategies accordingly.
